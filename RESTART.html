CASE

Card Design

YEAR

2022

DESCRIPTION

Through this deck of cards, the aim is to facilitate the integration and interaction of refugee children in their new host society. To achieve this goal, the working team designed cards, which display a series of images that can depict the feelings or concepts that the child wants to express. These cards can be juxtaposed with each other, so as to give the opportunity to formulate broader and more complex concepts that cannot be expressed through the use of a single card. Below the illustration is a blank space that is useful for describing through a word (in the native language of the host country) what the child wants to express when showing such a card, so as to teach him or her the basic vocabulary of the language.
