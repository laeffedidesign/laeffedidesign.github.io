TITOLO

Passi d'Arte Sul Campo

CLIENT

Personal Project

CASE

Editorial

YEAR

2023

DESCRIPTION

PASSI D’ARTE SUL CAMPO is a university project that aims to create a fanzine, a self-managed and limited edition magazine, focused on a specific theme. The main objective is to explore and present the rebranding of Italian football teams, i.e. the process of renewing and reworking the brands and visual identities of Italian football teams.The fanzine includes 3 Italian and 3 international graphic studios, selected for their experience and ability in the field of graphic design, more specifically in the art of reinterpreting in a creative and artistic way the logos and visual elements of the Italian football teams subject to rebranding.

Besides that, the project will also include 3 case studies of artworks. These case studies will focus on works of art that are inspired by the world of football or have football itself as a central theme. These works of art were analyzed and interpreted to highlight how football can be considered a source of inspiration and an artistic subject.

In summary, the project aims to explore the artistic and creative side of the world of Italian football through the rebranding of teams and the artistic interpretation of football-related works of art. The fanzine "Passi d'arte sul campo" represents an innovative initiative that merges two apparently distant worlds, graphic design and art, to offer a unique and original perspective on football culture.
