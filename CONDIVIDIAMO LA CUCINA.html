TITOLO

Condividiamo La Cucina

CLIENT

Banco Alimentare Torino

CASE

Packaging

YEAR

2023

DESCRIPTION

For this project, the Banco Alimentare of Turin has requested the creation of a product that succeeds in encouraging donations and collaboration between user and the Food Bank. As a response to this brief, the following was born "Let's Share the Kitchen." A project dedicated to everyday cooks, ready to experiment with new recipes and incentivized by the pleasure of performing a charitable action. By purchasing the box, one will be donated box with the same ingredients to a family. After the purchase, the donor will remove a postcard from the box containing a unique code, which will allow the donor to get in touch with the donor. Through an apparent division, a a bond will be created between the two parties, who will will cook the same recipe.
