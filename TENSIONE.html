DESCRIPTION

The main objective imposed was to make a modular seat and small table with reversible joints, all inspired by Stam's cantilever seats. The result obtained is a stainless steel frame and a leggo top, where the chair and small table fit into each other giving the possibility of adapting in the best way to the user's needs, occupying as little space as possible in the context in which they will be placed.
